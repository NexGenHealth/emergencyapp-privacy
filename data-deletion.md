# Data Deletion Instructions

**Family Emergency App by NexGenHealth**
*Last updated: March 18, 2026*

## How to Delete Your Data

All personal data stored by the Family Emergency App is kept **locally on your device only**. NexGenHealth does not store any of your data on external servers. You have full control over your data at all times.

### Delete All Data at Once

1. Open the **Family Emergency App**
2. Tap the **Settings** icon (gear icon)
3. Scroll to the **Data** section
4. Tap **Clear All Data**
5. Confirm the deletion when prompted

This immediately and permanently deletes all data stored by the app on your device.

### Delete Specific Data

#### Emergency Contacts
1. Open the app and navigate to **Emergency Contacts**
2. Select the contact you wish to remove
3. Tap **Delete** to remove that contact

#### Notification Preferences
1. Open the app and navigate to **Settings**
2. Tap **Notifications** to modify or reset your alert preferences

#### Revoke Permissions
1. Open your device's **Android Settings**
2. Go to **Apps** > **Family Emergency App** > **Permissions**
3. Revoke any permissions (Location, Contacts, SMS) at any time

## What Data Is Deleted

| Data Type | Deleted By "Clear All Data" | Automatic Deletion |
|-----------|----------------------------|-------------------|
| Emergency Contacts (names, phone numbers, emails) | Yes — immediately | No — stored until you delete |
| Notification Preferences and Groups | Yes — immediately | No — stored until you delete |
| GPS Location Cache | Yes — immediately | Auto-deleted after 24 hours |
| Notification History | Yes — immediately | Auto-deleted after 7 days |
| Message Queue (unsent SMS) | Yes — immediately | Cleared after successful delivery |

## What Data Is NOT Stored

NexGenHealth does **not** store any of your data on our servers. There is:

- No cloud backup of your data
- No account or profile to delete
- No server-side data retention
- No analytics or tracking data collected

Because all data is stored locally on your device, **uninstalling the app also deletes all data**.
